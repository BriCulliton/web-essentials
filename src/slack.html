Here's a quick overiew of what this integration will allow you to do.

This integration will post notifications into a Slack channel when you update and complete to-dos in a selected list.

What is Slack? Slack is real-time messaging, archiving and search for teams.

Connecting to Slack

Here's a quick guide on how to set up your Slack integration

  1. Sign into your Slack account and go to https://slack.com/integrations.
  2. Look for "Wunderlist" inside the list of "All Integrations" and click on "Add" on the right side.
  3. Select or create a Slack channel and click on "Add Wunderlist Integration".
  4. In the next step please "Authenticate with Wunderlist" and sign into your Wunderlist account (This has to be done once).
  5. Choose a list from your Wunderlist and click on "Save Integration".
